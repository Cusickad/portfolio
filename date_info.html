The Wedding of Michelle Myers and Adam Cusick

Michelle Myers is marrying Adam Cusick on Saturday, June 26, 2021. The ceremony will take place at 3 PM at St. Josaphat Ukrainian Catholic Church in Warren, Michigan. The reception will begin at 6 PM at Lafayette Grande Banquet Facility in Pontiac Michigan. The couple is registered at Target, Bed Bath and Beyond, and Amazon. Adam and Michelle request that this please be an adults-only event.

Ceremony at St. Josaphat

St. Josaphat Ukrainian Catholic Church is located at 26401 St Josaphat Dr, Warren, MI 48091. Although the ceremony starts at 3 PM, we ask you to arrive a few moments early. There is ample parking in the church's parking lot, and street parking is available as well. The ceremony will last roughly 1 hour.

Reception at Lafayette Grande

Lafayette Grande Banquet Facility is located at 1 Lafayette St, Pontiac, MI 48342. Parking may be found in several lots behind the building, and an employee of the facility will be on hand to guide you to a spot. There will be a pre-reception cocktail hour on the upper level of the Crystal Ballroom from 5 PM to 6 PM, with live music and an open bar. The reception proper will begin at 6 PM on the main floor of the Crystal Ballroom. Dinner will be served family-style, and there will be an open bar, desserts and late-night snacks.
